OpenSpades

Overview

OpenSpades is an open-source voxel-based first-person shooter game, inspired by the original Ace of Spades. It combines elements of construction, destruction, and combat, allowing players to build structures, dig tunnels, and engage in intense battles with firearms and explosives.

Gameplay

In OpenSpades, players join a team and compete against opponents in various game modes, including Team Deathmatch, Capture the Flag, and Zombie Survival. The objective of each mode varies, but generally involves eliminating enemy players, capturing flags, or surviving waves of zombies.

Players can use a variety of weapons, ranging from pistols and rifles to shotguns and grenades, to eliminate opponents and accomplish objectives. Additionally, the game features destructible environments, allowing players to reshape the battlefield by digging tunnels, building barricades, or demolishing structures.

Key Features:

  • Voxel-based graphics for unique visual style
  • Customizable maps and game modes
  • Support for single-player and multiplayer matches
  • Community-driven development with regular updates and mods
  • Accessible to players of all skill levels
